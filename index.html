Meet the Family

The Carter family lives in North Carolina and enjoys spending time together, volunteering, traveling, and supporting each other’s goals.

James Carter - Father

James works as an IT project manager. He enjoys cycling, grilling, and coaching youth soccer on weekends.

Maria Carter - Mother

Maria works part-time as a community art instructor. She also volunteers at the local library and helps organize school events.

Emily Carter - Oldest Child

Emily is a college freshman studying biology. She participates in the campus environmental club and works part-time at the student bookstore.

Noah Carter - Middle Child

Noah is in high school. He plays basketball, belongs to the robotics club, and recently received an academic achievement award in math.

Sophia Carter - Youngest Child

Sophia is in elementary school. She enjoys dance class, reading competitions, and drawing animals.

Family Pets

The Carters have two pets: Max, a friendly golden retriever, and Luna, a curious gray cat. Max loves going on walks, while Luna prefers sitting by the window and watching birds.

Last Family Vacation

Their last family vacation was to Myrtle Beach, South Carolina. The family spent time swimming, visiting the boardwalk, eating seafood, and playing mini golf. They also visited a local aquarium and took family photos near the beach at sunset.
